🐾 About Us

The story behind Tibby & Friends

Meet the Author

T. A. Barker

T.A. Barker is a children's author and the proud dog owner of Tibby, the lovable inspiration behind many of his heartwarming stories. Through the adventures of his animal friends, T.A. Barker brings big life lessons to little readers with warmth, humor, and heart.

🐾 🐾 🐾

His books focus on friendship, kindness, patience, inclusion, and the social-emotional skills children need to thrive. Whether his characters are learning to wait their turn, communicate in unique ways, solve problems together, or support a friend in need, each story gently models positive behaviors in a way that feels natural, relatable, and fun.

Inspired by Tibby and the joy animals bring into our lives, T.A. Barker believes stories are powerful tools for raising compassionate, confident kids. When he's not writing, he's dreaming up new adventures where friendship always wins and every character has something special to share.

🌟 Our Mission

Every Tibby & Friends book is crafted to bring smiles, teach gentle lessons, and make storytime something kids and parents look forward to together. We believe every child deserves a friend like Tibby.

📖 The Series

The Tibby & Friends series follows Tibby the dog and a cast of wonderful friends through colorful, imaginative adventures. Each book is designed for young readers with vibrant illustrations and simple, engaging storytelling.

All books are available on Amazon, with more titles being added to the series regularly!
